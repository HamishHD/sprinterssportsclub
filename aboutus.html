About us

Sprinters Sports Club is an athletics and fitness academy. Sprinters started informally in 2011, with 6 athletes. The success of these 6 athletes brought in more budding athletes and now we have trained over 300 athletes. Apart from athletes we also have national swimmers, badminton, squash, football and basketball players working on improving the general elements of fitness every sportsman needs. We have trained over 1000 candidates to prepare for Police Recruitment.

Our Goal:
  “Create and nurture athletes to participate at the International Level”

Our Vision:
  To improve the basic components required by sportspersons and spread fitness among everyone.
