The sport of athletics is an exclusive collection of sporting events that involve competitive running, jumping, throwing, and walking. The most common types of athletics competitions are track and field, road running, cross country running, and race walking. We provide coaching for all track and field events.


The training is conducted in 4 phases.

Phase 1: 1 June to 31 August

Phase 2: 1 September to 30 November

Phase 3: 1 December to 28 Feb

Phase 4: 1 March to 31 May


Training Schedule:

Monday, Wednesday,Friday: 5-6pm: University Pavilion, Marine Lines

Saturday: 6:30am: Girgaum Beach(Beach Training)/Malabar Hill (Hill training)

Dryland training is a form of athletic training which is practiced by some swimmers and skaters. These individuals do workouts on dry land or out of skates so that they can perform more effectively in the water or on the ice.

We provide dry land training to various clubs like Otters Club, Ozone Club, etc

We provide game specific training to sportsmen of sports like basketball, football, squash, badminton,tennis,etc. This includes improvement in motor components like strength, agility, endurance,etc

General fitness training works towards broad goals of overall health and well-being, rather than narrow goals of sport competition, larger muscles or concerns over appearance.
